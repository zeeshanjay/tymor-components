Typography System

Primary Space Grotesk

Space Grotesk

Modern, geometric sans-serif perfect for technology brands. Clean, readable, and distinctive.

Weights: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700

Best for: Headlines, Navigation, UI Elements

Secondary Inter

Inter

Versatile, highly legible sans-serif. Excellent for body text and interface design.

Weights: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700

Best for: Body Text, Forms, Documentation

Playfair Display

Elegant serif for luxury touch. Perfect for special headings and premium sections.

Weights: 400, 500, 600, 700

Best for: Hero Headlines, Quotes, Premium Content

Weights: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800

Best for: Branding, Marketing, Display
